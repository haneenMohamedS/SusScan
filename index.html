Types of Virus




Virus

Malware that attaches to clean files, spreads uncontrollably and causes widespread damage by corrupting or deleting them.

Ransomware

Encrypts a user's files and demands payment for the decryption key, often spread through phishing emails or infected websites.

Fileless Malware

Fileless malware operates directly in memory, without leaving traces on the file system, making detection and analysis more challenging.
